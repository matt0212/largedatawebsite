The Katana is the samurai's weapon of choice. It is a curved, single-edged blade with a long grip for two hands. It is renowned for its sharpness and speed.

Katana were used by samurai in practising several martial arts and modern martial artists still use a variety of katana. Martial arts such as Iaijutsu, battōjutsu, iaidō, kenjutsu, Shinkendo, kendo, Aikido, Ninjutsu.

The popularity of katana by samurai went up because of the changing nature of close-combat warfare. The quicker draw of the sword was perfect for fast-paced combat
