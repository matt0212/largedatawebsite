Ancient myth and modern stories are filled with enchanted swords that make even the most unlikely protagonist infinitely more awesome. Here are some memoral swords from some great stories.

The Power Sword, also referred to as the Sword of Grayskull, started out as a mystical object which Skeletor tries to obtain both halves and put them together in order to gain control over Castle Greyskull. The Power Sword then became the means by which Prince Adam transforms into He-Man.

The Master Sword is a sacred blade which evil ones may never touch.... Only one worthy of the title of "Hero of Time" can pull it from the Pedestal of Time. Link pulls the sword to save the Princess Zelda in one of Nintendo's best games.

Fashioned from pure silver, The Sword of Griffindor is inset with rubies, the stone that represents Gryffindor in the hour-glasses that count the house points at Hogwarts in the story of Harry Potter.
